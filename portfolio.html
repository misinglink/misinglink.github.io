DFS Demo

DFS is a Flask app that scrapes football data from several paid sources, pipelines the data in a NoSQL database, and then displays data a table manner in the dashboard part of the app. Included is a app to stack teams and analyze teams based on key metrics.

Examining Interaction Amongst Patrilineal Clans Using Differential Equations

This work was done as part of the SIMIODE differential equation competition. This year my partner and I decided to investigate the effects of interactions between patrlineal clans on genetic diversity in men. We applied principles in population modeling to examine how friendly or aggresive interations affected the population in a clan, thereby affecting genetic diversity.

Examining Conductivity in differnet kinds of Stones and Stone States

This research was done with ACC/UT-Austin Geology undergraduate research team. The goal of our research was to determine the relationship between physical properties and possible states of a rock and its electrical conductivity. My group tested the conductivity level in 4 different rock cores and saw how in changed based on stats like porosity, saturation, and ion content. Needless to say 4 rock cores didn't provide much statistical robustness, but the experience in a lab was priceless. .
