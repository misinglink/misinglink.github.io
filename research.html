    • Examining Conductivity in differnet kinds of Stones and Stone States

    Cesar Tolentino, Heather Garnder, Lia Schexnyder, Than Nguyen

    This research was done with ACC/UT-Austin Geology internship cohort. The goal of our research was to determine the relationship between physical properties and possible states of a rock and its electrical conductivity. My group tested the conductivity level in 4 different rock cores and saw how in changed based on stats like porosity, saturation, and ion content. Needless to say 4 rock cores didn't provide much statistical robustness, but the experience in a lab was priceless.
